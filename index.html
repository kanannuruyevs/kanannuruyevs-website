About Me

I started coding in C++ and Java during my Bachelor's studies, but wasn't interested at all, so most of the time, I got busy with doing research and deliver presentations at seminars based on lecture topics. After graduation, I became an IT intern, but my internship was cut short due to the COVID-19 pandemic, so that led me to work only as a freelancer, where I was up to create two PowerPoint slides for client’s specifications. Then, I initially faced several life challenges; sending proposals to clients after my first job ever didn't help, instead, I met with financial difficulties and disruptions to my future plans, such as education abroad that caused me to decline my application process.

I stayed determined and used the break time to explore potential career paths and refine my personal skills. In 2022, I discovered programming with important decision of my life to begin learning Python through an Android App and kept my keen interest in exploring other programming & markup languages, such as JavaScript, HTML & CSS. I was also prone to bother with the issues of the software, mostly related to Linux apps and extensions, which led me to increase my usage of GitHub by opening issues and making pull requests and engaging in reporting bugs, suggesting new features, correcting grammar mistakes in the documentation, etc. While my programming proficiency is currently at a basic level and I face challenges when writing code from scratch, I can comprehend programming structures and implement necessary code with the help of ChatGPT, Gemini and so on.
