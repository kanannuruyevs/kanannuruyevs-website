Visualizing My Career Options with a Venn Diagram

Kanan Nuruyev
• Jan 12, 2024

Welcome to my Venn Diagram! Here, we have three circles representing different sets and their intersections, designed to help visualize my career path.

The Components

The diagram consists of the following components:

  • Interests: This circle demonstrates artistic and philosophical interests, emphasizing creativity and deep thinking.
  • Skills: This circle showcases technical skills alongside nutritional expertise, demonstrating a multidisciplinary approach.
  • Needs: This circle encompasses financial and health needs, emphasizing effective communication for holistic solutions.

When the three circles overlap, it suggests a viable career option. In this case, programming appears to be the most suitable path.

Cognitive Functions (MBTI)

The project also visualizes the use of MBTI cognitive functions. Here's a breakdown of the circles and their corresponding functions:

  • First Circle (Fi - Introverted Feeling): Represents an individual's inner values and personal ethics.
  • Second Circle (Se/Ni - Extraverted Sensing & Introverted Intuition): Se focuses on gathering concrete information from the environment, while Ni helps recognize patterns and understand abstract concepts.
  • Third Circle (Te - Extraverted Thinking): Involves organizing the external world, making logical decisions, and achieving goals.

Intersections

By exploring the intersections of these circles, we unlock cognitive functions that reveal MBTI personality traits:

  • The intersection of the first and second circles gives rise to Fe (Extraverted Feeling), fostering empathy and harmonious interactions.
  • The intersection of the second and third circles reveals Si (Introverted Sensing), bringing attention to detail and practicality.
  • The intersection of the first and third circles uncovers Ti (Introverted Thinking), promoting logical analysis and problem-solving.
  • The intersection of all three circles represents Ne (Extroverted Intuition), encouraging innovation and exploration.

Conclusion

This exploration highlights a career path that integrates:

  • Artistic and philosophical insights to drive innovative solutions.
  • Technical skills applied in conjunction with nutritional expertise.
  • A holistic approach addressing financial, health, and communication needs.
  • An emphasis on empathy, attention to detail, logical analysis, and innovation.
